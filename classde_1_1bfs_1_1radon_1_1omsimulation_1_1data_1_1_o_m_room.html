void de.bfs.radon.omsimulation.data.OMRoom.calculateAttributes ( )

Calls the setters for the attributes Count, Median, Maxima and Deviation to re-calculate and update them. Call this method always after changes to the radon values. Note: It's not needed to call this after using the OMRoom.setValues() method as modifying values using the setter always triggers the re-calculation of attributes on its own.
